Pesach with Aishaudio

 
 

The sharp smell of freshly cleaned surfaces, freshly baked matzah, sweet wine and children's voices as we near the Pesach seder. Make this holiday a spiritual stretch as well with Torah from Jerusalem. May we feel the Pesach spring air bring us to places we've always longed for.

Introductory

  
 

Pesach Seder: The Wicked Son

KI 520 B
by Kahn, Rabbi Ari

The Haggadah is a document that reflects the ethic of education. As such, it needs to address each child’s unique needs. Rabbi Kahn examines the wicked son's good question, and the seemingly harsh response that followed. Hear a fascinating expose on the Christian view that Passover is not necessary due to the redemption of their savior to gain a unique perspective on the ideological struggle in modern generations.

Advanced

  
 

Pesach: Seeing Your Personal Exodus

 HT 520 D
by Heller, Rebbitzen Tziporah

When we speak of the Exodus, we often overlook the bigger picture. Rebbitzen Heller shows how leaving Egypt reflects the paths of our own lives, and why we would never have left of Mitrayim had God not pushed us. How does telling the Exodus story help us see the message in a way we can really get it?

Advanced

  
 

Pesach: Forging Our Identity

LZ 520 A
by Leff, Rabbi Zev

With intriguing insight to our Exodus from slavery to freedom, Rabbi Leff depicts the painful process of forging a nation and how it relates to shaking off our own personal enslavement.

Advanced

  
 

Haggadah: The Symbolism of Redemption

EA 525 A
by Elbaz, Rabbi Avraham

Rabbi Elbaz examines the symbolism of Seder night - from the first cup of wine till the last early morning song. Meet the confusing humble matzah, who melds both poverty and freedom into a beautiful picture. Find out the four quirks of this night, and on and on from a new dynamic speaker on AishAudio.

Introductory

  
 

Great Jewish Women # 14: Miriam

MX 639 N
by Mansour, Mrs. Bonnie

Miriam was born during a rough period in our history, and her name reflects how bitter it was. Yet even as a young child, her sense of belief that life would get better took action that no one else thought to do. This optimism and closeness to God stayed with her through crossing the Red Sea and her travels in the desert, leaving illumination where all seemed black.

Advanced

  
 

A Harmonious Pesach Seder

MC 525 B
by Morgenstern, Rabbi Chaim

Many parents of young children feel that a peaceful, meaningful Seder is beyond reach, where the Seder leaders turn into policemen. To make sure we give the Haggadah to the next generation in a way that all benefit, hear a number of great ideas and practical tips.

Advanced

  
 

"A'ggadah Tell Ya This Story": Part One

WG 525 A
by Wagensberg, Rabbi Abba

Rabbi Wagensberg continues with the highlights of the Pesach Seder. The four sons and how difference attitudes change our experience of Judaism. Learn how Hashem’s love is completely unconditional, and how our love for God elevated our Divine service. Find how the four matriarchs toughened us not to cave in to the pressure of Egypt - to earn us redemption. This class ends by exploring Chad Gadya and the souls journey.

 WN 790 A

Being 'constantly aware' sounds like a lot of hard work, but Judaism sees it differently. Learning what makes you tick is a most energizing voyage. and fascinating. So hop aboard this time-proven Jewish formula for unlocking all your potential.
