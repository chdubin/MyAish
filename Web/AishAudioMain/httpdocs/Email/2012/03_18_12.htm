Weekly Torah Portion: Vayikra

New Releases

Advanced

  
 

Chinuch: Roots of Growth #2 :Genetics of the Soul

 NY 626 B
by Neustadt, Rebbitzen Yitty

Did you know that just like physical traits are passed down to our offspring, so too spiritual traits - including your positive character traits? Rebbitzen Neustadt shows how overcoming ones negative inclination not only changes the world - but that your children will metamorphose from the growth that you make.

Introductory

  
 

Communicating Your Love

GV 615 B
by Gross, Rabbi Avraham

When we speak to others, how can we communicate in a way that perceives them as children of Hashem? What simple things can we do during our day to day relationships to open such a perception? Rabbi Gross gives an excellent definition of love and guides us on upgrading our vision by what we say, and how we react.

Advanced

  
 

Hidden Dangers in a Positive Environment

 SM 605
by Stern, Rabbi Moshe Aharon zt"l

Rabbi Stern examines a confusing correlation. We learn that when Torah observance increases, there is also a rise of accidentals murders - necessitating more cities of refuge. But shouldn't there be fewer accidental deaths if we're more careful to observe Torah laws? Hear the hidden dangers that prevail in a positive environment - and the remedy.

Advanced

  
 

Pesach Cleaning Made Easy

BY 521 A
by Berkovits, Rabbi Yitzchak

It's that time of year again - but hopefully this year your Pesach cleaning will be a lot easier. Rav Berkovits gives clear, simple guidelines to minimize the 'Pesach dread.' He spells out what is essential - and to what extent. This all-in-one review combines bottom line halachos with great practical advice for eliminating unnecessary tasks. Get to your Seder table in peace this year - to better enjoy the Hag.

Intr/Adv

  
 

Vayikra: Animal Sacrifices Today?

 KY 024 B
by Kirzner, Rabbi Yitzchak zt"l

Is there anything less 21st century then to offer animal sacrifices? Rabbi Kirzner delves into our body-soul conflict by examining the different ways we picture ourselves. The clear model of animal sacrifices dramatically shows what the inner struggle for harmony is all about.
