Weekly Torah Portion: Balak

New Releases

Introductory

  
 

Halacha: The Science of Spirituality

 BY 700 A
by Berkovits, Rabbi Yitzchak

Can the laws of the spiritual dimension be clearly defined? Does God really care how exact we are about Jewish law? Why do Orthodox Jews say that some rabbis are legitimate and others not? Other subjects discussed include the role of the soul, God's relationship to His creations, and that passionate Jewish thirst for justice.

Advanced

  
 

The Fantasy Between Myself & My Soul

 JO 532 G
by Juravel, Mrs. Chana

We can often convince ourselves to resist our desire for things with tricks and mind games. How possible is it to miraculously break through such negativity? Hear how we erect internal walls between ourselves and our souls, and how tears can help with the process.

Advanced

  
 

Vegitarianism

 FE 640
by Feiner, Rabbi Eytan

Knowing that what we eat affects us is only a part of the reason we avoid certain foods. When and why were we given permission to kill animals for food, and why are we told how important it is to eat meat on holy days? Rabbi Feiner’s fast paced style covers many other topics including kindness to the animal kingdom and animal ownership.

Advanced

  
 

Words That Hurt

 MC 614 F
by Morgenstern, Rabbi Chaim

Words are far more powerful than we imagine. Rabbi Morgenstern guides us to avoid inflicting pain on others with speech - words which to us may sound so harmless. Topics include reminding others of their past, being sensitive to a child's feelings, and checking the price an item in a store, etc. This class is highly informative and all-encompassing.

Introductory

  
 

Balak - Stabbing at the Jewish Core

KI 040 C
by Kahn, Rabbi Ari

How do non-Jews view the Jews? Rabbi Kahn analyzes the qualities that Abraham bestowed on his descendants, and how Balak desperately tried to undermine their influence and crush their strength of character. Through which weakness was he able to cause the Jewish nation a terrible fall, and why was his plan able to work?
