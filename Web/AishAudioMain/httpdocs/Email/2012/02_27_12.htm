Weekly Torah Portion: Tetzaveh

Purim

New Releases

Advanced

  
 

How is Amalek Affecting Us?

 TE 555 A
by Tauber, Rabbi Ezriel

When we look back and count the major enemies of the Jewish nation, we find that Pharaoh and Amalek head the list. Rabbi Tauber points out the underlying differences between them, and what steps we are able to take to counteract their harmful influence.

Advanced

  
 

Truth and Imagery

HT 909 B
by Heller, Rebbitzen Tziporah

Sometimes the yetzer hara approaches us with logical arguments that are easy to deflect. But how well can we deal with desires that hit us with urgency and emotion force? Hear a fascinating lecture on our powers of imagery and its capacity to fight negative impulses. Rebbitzen Heller also flips the concept by examining the illusionary portraits of greatness we sometimes construct in our minds.

Introductory

  
 

Jewish Philosophy 101:
#8 Don't Corrupt Your Brain with Illogical Thoughts

BY 807 H
by Berkovits, Rabbi Yitzchak

In each person's search to find a suitable ideology to live with, many ideas and opinions are entertained and evaluated. However, at what point do excess or unfounded pieces of information become counter-productive?

Advanced

  
 

The Truth About Lying

 MC 615 E
by Morgenstern, Rabbi Chaim

George Washington's famous cherry tree aside, is the commandment to shun falsehood so straightforward? Rabbi Morgenstern gives us guidelines while underscoring the incredible value of being a person of truth. Topics include keeping your word, keeping the peace, and standing in your integrity despite all external pressures. Also hear remarkable stories of courageous people who succeeded in maintaining their honesty.

Introductory

  
 

Destiny # 4 - Edge of History

KE 793 D
by Spiro, Rabbi Ken

Our tumultuous times indicate that we are near the end of history. What specific things are we expected to accomplish in the world, and how well are we doing the job? Rabbi Spiro ties together the roles of Judaism and the goals of the universe by exploring the Torah's outlook on the 'end of days' and the what is to occur at that time.
